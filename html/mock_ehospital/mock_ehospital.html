Welcome to eHospital

This demo simulates the national digital health ecosystem including OPD, appointment booking, and ABHA integration.

Patient Registration (OPD & Appointments)

Register for outpatient departments or casualty with the ability to book, reschedule, or cancel appointments digitally.

Admission, Discharge & Transfer (IPD)

Simulate in-patient admission and manage services like treatment, discharge and ward transfer during a hospital stay.

Lab Reports & Diagnostics

Generate or view sample lab reports as a part of the eHospital record integration with ABHA health IDs.
