📘 DigiLocker Overview

🧠 What is DigiLocker?

DigiLocker is a free and secure online locker provided by the Government of India. It allows you to store important documents digitally such as Aadhaar card, PAN card, Driving License, Mark sheets, Certificates, and Ration card. You can access these anytime using your phone or computer.

💡 Why is DigiLocker Important?

You don’t need to carry physical documents. It is accepted by banks, schools, offices, and for travel. No fear of losing documents. It helps reduce paper usage and is eco-friendly.

🎯 How will DigiLocker Help You?

Save time and effort — just show your digital document. Easy to download, share, or show documents anytime. You can verify your identity online using it. It gives rural and urban users equal access to government services.
